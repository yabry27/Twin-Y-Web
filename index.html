About Us

Built for students, offices, creators, and growing businesses.

Founded in 2025 by a dedicated team of visionary young shareholders, Twin Y Stationery and Digital Solutions PLC began as an ambitious startup built from the ground up. Over the past year, we have evolved from a nascent venture into a comprehensive business partner, establishing a strong reputation for excellence, reliability, and innovation within the competitive Ethiopian market. Our rapid growth is a testament to our commitment to quality and our ability to adapt swiftly to the changing needs of our clients.

At the core of our operations is a dual-focus strategy that seamlessly integrates essential physical office supplies with cutting-edge digital services. We recognize that modern businesses require more than just hardware; they need integrated systems that function reliably every day. By combining high-quality stationery distribution with sophisticated digital solutions, we provide a one-stop-shop approach that streamlines procurement and enhances overall operational efficiency for our diverse clientele.

Looking toward the future, we have set an ambitious yet achievable long-term vision to become the leading technology and digital solutions provider in Ethiopia by 2035. This goal is driven by our desire to serve as a catalyst for local digital transformation. We are not merely aiming for growth; we are striving to become an essential partner for organizations navigating the complexities of the modern digital economy, ensuring they have the tools necessary to thrive in an increasingly connected world.

Ultimately, Twin Y Stationery and Digital Solutions PLC is dedicated to bridging the critical gap between traditional business needs and modern operational excellence. By investing in our team, staying ahead of technological trends, and maintaining a customer-centric focus, we are positioning ourselves to be a cornerstone of Ethiopia's evolving business landscape. We are proud of the progress we have made in our first year and remain steadfast in our mission to empower businesses through reliable, innovative, and forward-thinking solutions.
